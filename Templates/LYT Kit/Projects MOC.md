# Projects MOC
There are excellent ways to manage projects and tasks with linked frameworks. However, the LYT opinion is that most projects need *less* maintenance work, not more. Don't micro-manage yourself. Keep it spartan. Give yourself a broad direction and let your own ingenuity surprise you. That's why the following are deliberately minimal project "cover pages".
